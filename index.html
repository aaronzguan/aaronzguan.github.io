About me

I am a software engineer at TuSimple. I graduated with my M.S. degree from the Robotic Systems Development at The Robotics Institute, Carnegine Mellon University (CMU). I received my Bachelor of Engineering with first class honor from Department of Electronic & Information Engineering, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I have also studied at McGill University as an exchange student.

I was a self-driving perception intern at NVIDIA in 2020 summer. Prior to CMU, I was a deep learning research intern at Mech-Mind Robotics. I also worked at HSBC, Hong Kong for about one year as software engineer. I have fortunately worked with Prof. Chen Li in Terradynamics Lab at LCSR, Johns Hopkins University, Prof. Kin-Man Lam at PolyU, and Dr. Yanan Cao at Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
