About me

I'm a 4th-year undergraduate student in Department of Electronic & Information Engineering, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). I have also studied at McGill University as exchange student and FH Aachen University of Applied Science as ROS summer student. During my undergraduate studies, I am awarded Best GPA Award, Dean’s Honors List, Wong Tit-Shing Student Scholarship and HKSAR Government Scholarship, etc. I will join The Robotics Institute at Carnegine Mellon University as a master student in Fall 2019.

I have fortunately worked with Prof. Chen Li in Terradynamics Lab at Johns Hopkins University, Prof. Kin-Man Lam at PolyU, and Dr. Yanan Cao at Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I was also a Software Development Engineer at HSBC, where I supported the largest electronic payment system EPS in Hong Kong.

I am a robotics enthusiast. I have more than 2 years experience in robotics and have developed 4 autonomous robots, including 3 robotic cars and 1 quadrotor. All my projects are listed in Project Page. I am experienced in signal processing, embedded system development, computer vision, and machine learning. I am particularly interested in how robot can operate efficiently and safely in a human-involved environment and how they can better assist and serve people, especially disadvantaged people, in their daily lives. I am looking for a summer internship in robotics starting May 2019. Please contact me if you find me suitable for any position.
